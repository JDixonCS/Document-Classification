Dr. D. M. Macdonald, M.D., F.R.C.P.E. 9th Edition. Edinburgh; E. & D. Livingstone, 1925. Pp. 226. Price, 3s. net.

In a miniature volume which will readily go into the vest pocket, the author has condensed no less than 551 prescriptions with directions for use. They are arranged according to the diseases for which they are intended.
There is a great deal of information on prescribing, a table of doses, a vocabulary, diet tables, etc. This handy and cheap volume will be found very useful by students and doctors. It has reached the 9th edition and is becoming increasingly popular.
